VISIONS

Why are we running?

Drea

@AndreaNatividad

Making a difference in the life of another person is the most rewarding feeling in the world. I want to be part of the SSG so that I can have an impact on my peers. I want to strike a balance between learning and having fun. I can only hope that my schoolmates believe in my plans and my enthusiasm for service, and that they will be eager to pitch in and contribute to the numerous things we will be doing for our school and the communities surrounding us.

Kyle

@KyleGuerra

To me, the world’s greatest satisfaction is reaching down and lifting someone up. You never know how big of an impact you are making in someone’s life. I believe that I should do everything in my power to help the ones in need. It is me who they rely on, and I must not fail them even if that means sacrificing my sake for theirs. If we all pitch in and help others, we can make an impact in society.

Toti

@LouiseGegabine

And when time comes where we feel like we do not have anyone with us. SOAR will always be there. To those who are blinded by the sweetest promises, those who are too good to be true, we will stand by You. I am running to be the voice of the voiceless, the voice for the silent, and those who are silenced. I campaign for equity before equality, balanced and a premise of justice. I am Louise and I don’t just fly, I SOAR high.

Ayi

@A'ieshaDayao

"Leadership is not about titles, positions, or flowcharts. It is about one life influencing another." I wanted to run as a student leader because I believe that I have the ability to lead and help those people around me. I wanted to help the people who needs help and I believe that running as a student leader is a great opportunity for that. I also wanted to give voice to the people as well as express my stands and arguements to everyone.

Aljen

@AljenAguirre

I am running for this position because I believe in my abilities and skills that I can be a good leader. I will not only be a good leader, but also a good listener and the voice of my fellow cadets. I am aware of my abilities and what I can provide to the position for which I am running. I am confident that I will do everything in my power to put my advocacy into action.

Sam

@SamanthaRoxas

There’s nothing more fulfilling to me than to be able to make a change in someone’s life. With this platform I know that I can lead the student body with courage and strength. I make the commitment to do everything in my ability to serve as your representative and to listen to any concerns that anyone may have. I have a burning desire to help and be a strong voice for those who don’t feel heard. At the end of the day it is not the position or the title that matters to me, but the impact and change that I am able to contribute to this community.
